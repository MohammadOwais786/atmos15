HOSPITALITY

All these past three years of exponential growth have made ATMOS a fest of astronomical proportions. But, it was never the big numbers that drove it. What made it all worthwhile was to see people having a great time even during nerve-wrecking events, and the guests leaving with fulfilled smiles promising to return. That's the plan this time as well.

Though ours is a huge campus, you'll never feel lost among the crowd. Apart from each and every Bitsian, we'll have dedicated teams to ensure that and help you in every way possible. We'll strive to make sure that you have a comfortable and memorable stay here.

Hope to see you at Atmos'15.

REGISTERATIONS

Apart from the convinent online mode, option to register on arrival is also open. The general registeration fee for ATMOS is Rs.250 per person. This will entitle you to an Atmos ID which can be used to participate in most of the events. But please note that there are some events and workshops which have separate charges. Information regarding the same is available on the concerned site-section.

ACCOMODATION

The guests will be sharing dormitory style rooms with mattresses and buckets provided. Charges will be- Rs150 per day per person, and Rs450 for 4 days.
